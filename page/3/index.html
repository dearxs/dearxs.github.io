How to live our life

Posted on 2020-02-27 In 翻译 Disqus:

“不是勇敢不勇敢的问题，是对什么能说什么不能说已经失去了判断的问题；也不是后不后悔，值不值得的问题，是所有人、所有事情都不可能再倒回到一个月前的问题；不是节目要怎么做下去的问题，是人（我自己或者所有人）要怎么继续生活下去的问题。 ”

——张之琪，剩余价值主播 von Twitter

It is not a problem of being brave or not, but a problem of we not knowing what is or is not allowed to be discussed; it is not a question of ‘regret or not’, ‘worthy or not’ either, whatever we say or do, we can never travel back to one month ago. It is not a problem of how our podcast will run in the future, but a problem of how the people want to live their life in the future.

by Zhang Zhiqi, host of “Surplus Value” Podcast
