关于性的一些话

Posted on 2019-09-02 Edited on 2020-03-20 In 翻译 Disqus:

所有引言来自 Goodreads。
翻译：PlayerUnknown

“Everything in the world is about sex except sex. Sex is about power.” ― Oscar Wilde

世上万物皆与性有关，唯独性本身除外。性关乎权力。

“Man may have discovered fire, but women discovered how to play with it.” ― Candace Bushnell, Sex and the City

男人发现了火，女人发现了如何玩火。

“Fighting for peace is like screwing for virginity.” ― George Carlin

为了和平而斗争，就好比为了贞操去做爱。

“Writing is like sex. First you do it for love, then you do it for your friends, and then you do it for money.” ― Virginia Woolf

写作如同性爱。起先为了爱，然后为了朋友，然后为了钱。

“Sex is part of nature. I go along with nature.” ― Marilyn Monroe

性爱是本性的一部分，我选择追随本性。

“The difference between sex and love is that sex relieves tension and love causes it.” ― Woody Allen

性和爱之间的区别在于，性让人放松，爱让人紧张。

“I don’t know the question, but sex is definitely the answer.” ― Woody Allen

我不知道问题是什么，但答案肯定是性。

“It’s not true that I had nothing on. I had the radio on.” ― Marilyn Monroe

说我身无一物不太准确，我身上有电波。
